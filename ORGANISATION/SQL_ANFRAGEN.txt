get an video group by id:

    SELECT title, video_group_id, isMovies
    FROM video_group
    WHERE video_group_id = ?;

get entries of video groups by cideo group id:

    SELECT entity_id
    FROM video_group_member
    WHERE video_group_id = ?;

JOINED get members of a video group:

    SELECT title, video_group_id, isMovies, entity_id
    FROM video_group as vg
    JOIN video_group_member as vgm on vgm.video_group_id = vg.video_group_id
    WHERE video_group_id = ?;


get an entity entry with id:

    SELECT title, picture
    FROM entity
    WHERE entity_id = ?;
